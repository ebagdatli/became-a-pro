About Dataset
This dataset comprises videos captured from a consistent POV, showcasing individuals performing sets of push-ups. It is meticulously organized into two folders: 'Correct' for accurately executed push-ups and 'Incorrect' for flawed performances. Utilizing MediaPipe, each video has been processed to generate .npy files containing recorded body keypoints, facilitating detailed analysis. Designed specifically for sequence model classification, such as LSTM, this dataset aims to enable the accurate classification of push-up execution as either correct or incorrect, offering valuable insights for fitness tracking and coaching applications.
